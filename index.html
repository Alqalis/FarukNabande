Birnin Kebbi–Kalgo–Bunza Student Development Forum

Introduction

The Birnin Kebbi–Kalgo–Bunza Student Development Forum is a collaborative initiative aimed at empowering students and graduates from the Birnin Kebbi, Kalgo, and Bunza local government areas. Our goal is to create a platform that fosters academic excellence, leadership, and community development among youth in these communities.

Our Mission

To provide students and graduates with opportunities for personal growth, skills development, mentorship, and networking, enabling them to contribute positively to their communities and beyond.

Our Vision

To become the leading student and graduate forum in the region, recognized for empowering youth, driving educational advancement, and promoting sustainable community development.

Our Ambition

  1. Facilitate career readiness and professional development programs
  2. Support youth entrepreneurship and innovation
  3. Encourage community service and active citizenship
  4. Build a strong network of students, graduates
  5. Professionals committed to the progress of Birnin Kebbi, Kalgo, and Bunza
